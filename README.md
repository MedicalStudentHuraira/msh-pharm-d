## Pharm-D Academic Archive

A comprehensive repository dedicated to the five-year Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm-D) curriculum. This archive serves as a central hub for lecture notes, study plans, and academic resources, covering the core pillars of pharmaceutical sciences and clinical practice.
## Project Overview

This repository is designed to bridge the gap between classroom lectures and independent study. It contains organized materials that follow the standard 5-year Pharm-D trajectory, focusing on "real academia"—practical insights, detailed pathology, and clinical applications.
## Repository Structure

The content is organized by academic year to ensure easy navigation:

    Year 1: Foundations

        Organic & Inorganic Chemistry, Physiology, and Anatomy.

    Year 2: Principles of Pharmacy

        Pharmaceutics (Physical Pharmacy), Pharmacognosy, and Microbiology.

    Year 3: Mechanisms & Action

        Pharmacology and Therapeutics I, Pathology, and Dispensing Pharmacy.

    Year 4: Clinical Specialization

        Clinical Pharmacy, Hospital Pharmacy, and Advanced Pharmacology.

    Year 5: Advanced Practice

        Pharmaceutical Technology, Forensic Pharmacy, and Clinical Clerkships.

## Key Contents

    Lecture Notes: Transcribed and summarized notes from core university modules.

    Reference Materials: Real world semister assest's

    Assessment Prep: practical, lab works etc

## Getting Started

    Browse by Year: Navigate to the folder corresponding to your current professional year.

    Search by Subject: Use GitHub's file finder to search for specific topics like "Hypersensitivity" or "Pharmacokinetics."
